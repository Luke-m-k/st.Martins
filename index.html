St Martins De Porres Catholic Church.

Welcome

The name "St. Martin's Catholic Church" is typically derived from the veneration of St. Martin of Tours, a popular Catholic saint known for his piety and charitable acts. Many Catholic parishes adopt the name "St. Martin's" in honor of him. The specific history of each St. Martin's Catholic Church may vary, but generally, the establishment of these churches can be traced back to the spread of Catholicism in different regions. Catholic missionaries and settlers often founded churches in new areas as part of their evangelization efforts. Learn More

How to find us.

St Martin's De Porres Catholic church is located along Kiganjo road which is on the left side of Thika SuperHighway as your heading towards Thika from Nairobi.
